# Pairly Content Policy - 18+ Platform Rules

## Platform Age Requirement

Pairly is an **18+ only platform**. All users must be 18 years of age or older to create an account, view content, or interact with other users.

## Prohibited Content

The following types of content are **strictly prohibited** and will result in immediate removal and potential account suspension:

### 1. Sexual Content (Zero Tolerance)
- **Nudity**: Any depiction of exposed genitals, breasts, or buttocks
- **Striptease/Erotic Dancing**: Content depicting removal of clothing in a sexually suggestive manner
- **Pornographic Material**: Any sexually explicit imagery, videos, or animations
- **Sexual Acts**: Depiction or simulation of sexual activities
- **Sexual Solicitation**: Offering or requesting sexual services, escorting, or prostitution
- **Erotic Messaging**: Text conversations intended for sexual arousal or gratification

### 2. Minor Safety (Immediate Escalation)
- Any content depicting, referencing, or involving minors in any context
- Content that sexualizes, endangers, or exploits individuals under 18
- **This triggers immediate platform removal, law enforcement notification, and NCMEC reporting**

### 3. Other Prohibited Content
- Illegal goods or services
- Violence, gore, or graphic injury
- Hate speech, harassment, or discrimination
- Spam, scams, or fraudulent schemes
- Copyright infringement
- Impersonation or identity fraud

## Allowed Content

Pairly supports genuine connection and dating content that is:

✅ **Non-Sexual Dating Content**
- Profile photos showing face and appropriate attire
- Personal interests, hobbies, and lifestyle content
- Dating preferences and relationship goals
- Conversation starters and icebreakers

✅ **PG-Rated Affection**
- Hand-holding, hugging, or cheek kissing (non-explicit)
- Romantic but non-sexual expressions
- Compliments and flirtation that don't cross into explicit territory

✅ **Creative Expression**
- Art, photography, and creative content (non-sexual)
- Music, dance (non-erotic), and performance art
- Educational or informational content

## Content Moderation Process

### 1. Pre-Upload Screening
- All text and images are analyzed before publication
- High-confidence explicit content is **blocked immediately**
- Suspicious content enters **quarantine** for review

### 2. Quarantine Review
- Content scoring between 0.50-0.85 is quarantined
- Automated deeper analysis performed
- Human moderator review for edge cases
- Decision: Publish or Remove

### 3. User Reporting
- Users can report violations via in-app reporting
- Reports are reviewed within 24-48 hours
- Repeat violators face escalating penalties

### 4. Enforcement Actions

**First Violation:**
- Content removal
- Warning notification
- 24-hour posting restriction

**Second Violation:**
- Content removal
- 7-day account suspension
- Required policy acknowledgment

**Third Violation:**
- Permanent account termination
- Content deletion
- IP/device ban consideration

**Severe Violations (Sexual Content, Minors):**
- Immediate permanent ban
- Law enforcement notification (if applicable)
- No appeal process

## Appeal Process

Users may appeal moderation decisions (except severe violations):

1. Submit appeal within 14 days
2. Provide context and explanation
3. Human moderator review (3-5 business days)
4. Final decision communicated via email

## Legal Compliance

Pairly complies with:
- **COPPA**: No users under 13 (we require 18+)
- **FOSTA-SESTA**: Zero tolerance for sexual solicitation
- **CSAM Laws**: Mandatory reporting of child exploitation
- **DMCA**: Copyright takedown procedures
- **GDPR/CCPA**: User data privacy and deletion rights

## Moderator Standards

Our moderation team follows:
- Consistent policy application
- Cultural sensitivity training
- Trauma-informed content review
- No tolerance for bias or discrimination
- Regular policy updates based on emerging trends
